## Overview

Affinity Photo Professional Suite is a complete professional photo editing platform that serves photographers and designers with the editing depth, batch capability, and format support needed for production-quality image work at a fraction of the cost of subscription alternatives. It supports PSD file compatibility for seamless collaboration with teams using Adobe tools, and its native file format preserves every layer, adjustment, and effect for future editing. The one-time purchase model means that the software remains fully functional indefinitely without ongoing payment.

HDR merge and tone mapping combine bracketed exposures into high dynamic range images with full creative control over the tone mapping process, producing HDR images that range from photorealistic to dramatic depending on creative intent. Ghost removal handles moving subjects between bracketed frames, and the tone mapping persona provides real-time preview of every adjustment. The merge algorithm handles large exposure brackets with the precision needed for architectural and landscape photography where dynamic range routinely exceeds sensor capability.

Focus merge stacking combines multiple images captured at different focus distances into a single image with extended depth of field, enabling macro and product photography with front-to-back sharpness that single exposures cannot achieve. The alignment algorithm handles the slight shift between frames that occurs when refocusing, and the merge boundary detection produces clean transitions between focus regions. Output images retain the full resolution and bit depth of the source captures.

360-degree image editing supports equirectangular panoramic images with tools that handle the distortion characteristics of spherical projection, enabling direct editing of 360 content without specialized separate software. The live 360 preview shows edits applied in the spherical context, and retouching tools automatically account for projection distortion near the poles. Export options produce files compatible with all major VR and 360 platforms.

Affinity Photo Professional Suite is the photo editing platform for professionals who reject the subscription model and want to own their creative tools outright, with the editing depth and production capability that makes the subscription alternative unnecessary. Its professional-grade results, one-time pricing, and continuous free updates demonstrate that world-class creative software does not require a recurring fee.

## Performance & Integration

Affinity Photo is engineered around macOS technologies that handle performance-critical work efficiently. Tasks run in the background without slowing down the rest of your system — you can keep working while Affinity Photo processes operations quietly behind the scenes.

The app integrates deeply with the macOS ecosystem out of the box:

- Launch workflows directly through **Spotlight**
- Preview files instantly with **Quick Look**
- Trigger actions from any app via the **Services menu**
- Fully compatible with **Shortcuts** for automated workflows
